LiU CTF

The LiTHe Hax CTF Event is an exciting competition taking place on March 8, bringing cybersecurity enthusiasts together to test their skills and tackle challenging Capture the Flag tasks. We host this event to encourage cybersecurity awareness within the student community, as it is both highly relevant and incredibly fun!

A total of 30 teams will be able to compete. Each team can consist of 2-5 participants, of which one must be a member in LiTHe Hax. Free food will be provided to each participant.

All non-student participants have to bring their own laptops, but it's recommended that every participant brings their own, since we may not be able to provide Kali Linux images on site.

Date: 17:15-23:00, March 8th, 2025 (Food will be served 16:15)

Location: Brandväggen, Bakdörren and ISYtan (Linköping University, Sweden)

CTF Platform: ctf.lithehax.se
